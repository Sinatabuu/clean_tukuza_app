Jesus, also known as Jesus Christ, is a central figure in Christianity and is believed to be the son of God. He is considered the savior of mankind and his teachings form the basis of the Christian faith. Jesus is believed to have been born in Bethlehem, raised in Nazareth, and ultimately crucified in Jerusalem. Christians believe that he was resurrected from the dead and ascended into heaven. Jesus is often referred to as the "Son of God," the "Lamb of God," and the "Prince of Peace." His life and teachings are recorded in the New Testament of the Bible.
