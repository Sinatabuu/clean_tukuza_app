Jesus is a central figure in Christianity, believed by Christians to be the Son of God and the savior of humanity. He is said to have been born in Bethlehem, performed miracles, preached about God's love and forgiveness, and ultimately died on the cross to atone for the sins of humanity. Christians believe that Jesus rose from the dead three days after his crucifixion, proving his power over death and offering salvation to all who believe in him. He is often referred to as Jesus Christ or simply as Jesus.
